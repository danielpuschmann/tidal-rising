About the Author

Heather Puschmann is a New Zealand-born fantasy author whose stories blend rich worldbuilding, political intrigue, slow-burn romance, and emotionally driven characters. After spending fifteen years in the fashion industry, Heather developed a deep appreciation for atmosphere, visual storytelling, and the small details that reveal character.
